Our story

A formula passed from a grandmother, kept by a mother, brewed by a daughter.

In 2008, my mother Nirmala took the herbal remedies her own mother had taught her — slow oil infusions, hand-blended powders, soaps poured in tiny batches — and quietly began making them for friends in our neighbourhood in Anna Nagar.

Word spread. Recipes were refined. Sixteen years later, the kitchen counter has become a small workshop, but nothing else has changed: the same 21 herbs, the same 21-day brew, the same hands.

"We didn't want a beauty brand. We wanted to keep a recipe alive."
Jeevitha · Founder
Sourcing

From the Godavari belt to your bathroom shelf — never more than four hands in between.

Every herb in our formulary is traceable. We source directly from Girijan tribal cooperatives and small farms across coastal Andhra Pradesh, where the soil is pesticide-free and the air carries the Bay of Bengal. No middlemen. No shortcuts. Every batch is documented from field to finished bottle.
