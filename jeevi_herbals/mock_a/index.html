Secrets of the Godavari Wilderness

Vetiver Root

Known as 'Khus', this deep-growing wild grass root cools skin, retains moisture, and provides a deep, earthy, wood-smoke scent.

  • Refreshes irritated skin
  • Balances oil secretions
  • Deeply grounding scent
🧱

Multani Mitti

Pure fuller's earth clay forged in volcanic ash, perfect for absorbing excess oils and drawing out toxic impurities from pores.

  • Absorbs deep impurities
  • Brightens tanned complexions
  • Tightens saggy pores
🍠

Kasthuri Manjal

Wild jungle turmeric that combats skin blemishes and acne bacteria, providing a soft natural glow without yellow staining.

  • Lightens spots & scars
  • Natural antibacterial
  • Evens out skin tones

Karuppu Kavuni

An ancient black rice seed variety packed with anthocyanins to feed hair follicles and protect skin cells against environmental aging.

  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Soothes damaged follicles
  • Improves hair texture

Kuppaimeni

Indian Acalypha, a wild leafy weed revered in traditional herbalism to heal rashes, allergies, and stubborn bacterial acne.

  • Calms allergic rashes

  • Speeds skin healing
