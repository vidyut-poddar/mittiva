Quick answers

Before you write

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship from our Chennai workshop within 48 hours. Delivery typically takes 3–6 business days across India, depending on your pincode.

Can I return or exchange a product?

For hygiene reasons we can't accept returns on opened products. If your item arrives damaged, send a photo within 48 hours and we'll send a free replacement. Full details on the shipping & returns page.

Do you ship internationally?

Not yet from the website, but we do ship internationally on request. Write to us with your address and we'll quote.

Are your products safe during pregnancy?

Our products are made from food-grade and traditionally used herbs, but please consult your doctor before starting any new product during pregnancy or while nursing.

Do you do wholesale or stocking?

Yes. We supply to a small number of carefully chosen stores. Email wholesale@jeeviherbals.com with your shop details.
