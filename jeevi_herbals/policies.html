Privacy policy

We collect the minimum information needed to run our shop. We do not sell your data to anyone. Ever.

What we collect

  • To place an order: name, shipping address, phone number, email, payment information (processed by our payment partner — we never see your card details).
  • To respond to enquiries: whatever you send us via the contact form or email.
  • For the newsletter: just your email address. Nothing else.
  • Automatically: basic analytics (page views, device type, country) using privacy-friendly tools that don't track you across other websites.

What we do with it

  • Process and ship your order.
  • Reply to your messages.
  • Send you the newsletter, only if you signed up. Unsubscribe in one click from any email.
  • Improve the website based on aggregate, anonymous patterns.

What we will never do

  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
  • Share your data with advertisers.
  • Send you marketing you didn't ask for.
  • Store your card details on our servers — payment is processed end-to-end by our PCI-DSS compliant payment partner.

Your rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@jeeviherbals.com and we'll respond within 7 working days.

Children

Our website is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify newsletter subscribers by email and post the updated effective date above.

Shipping & returns

Shipping within India

  • Free shipping on orders above ₹1,499.
  • Flat ₹60 shipping on orders below ₹1,499.
  • Orders ship from our Chennai workshop within 48 hours (Mon–Sat, excluding public holidays).
  • Typical delivery: 3–6 business days, depending on your pincode.
  • You'll receive a tracking link by email and SMS when your order ships.

International shipping

We don't currently offer international shipping through the website, but we do ship internationally on request. Write to hello@jeeviherbals.com with your address for a quote. Customs and import duties are the customer's responsibility.

Returns

For hygiene reasons we can't accept returns on opened personal-care or wellness products. We will, however, replace any product that:

  • Arrived damaged or leaking — send photos within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Is not what you ordered (our fault — send a photo and we'll replace it free of charge).
  • Arrived after the printed best-before date.

Email hello@jeeviherbals.com within 48 hours of delivery with your order number, the issue, and 1–2 photos. We'll respond within 3 working days. If a replacement is approved, we'll arrange a reverse pick-up at no cost to you.

Refunds

In cases where a replacement isn't possible (out of stock, discontinued), we will issue a full refund to the original payment method within 7–10 working days.

What we can't replace

  • Products that have been used or opened (unless damaged or defective on arrival).
  • Requests made more than 48 hours after delivery.
  • Products without their original packaging or batch label.

Cookies

We use two kinds of cookies on this site:

Essential cookies

These run the cart, remember your filter preferences during a session, and let you log into your account. The site won't work properly without them.

Analytics cookies (optional)

If you accept, we set a small first-party cookie that helps us understand which products and pages people are interested in, in aggregate. We don't use any third-party advertising cookies and we don't track you across other websites. You can decline at any time using the banner at the bottom of the page.

Changed your mind? Clear your cookies for this site in your browser settings and the banner will reappear so you can make a different choice.
